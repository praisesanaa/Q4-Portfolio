What are Wireless Based Systems?

Wireless Systems use radio frequency electromagnetic waves to send signals.


What is the function of Wireless Based Systems?

Wireless Systems use radio waves to move information through the air. Wireless routers have to be connected with either copper or Fiber Optic cable before they send and receive information wirelessly. Wireless connections use Wireless Access Points (WAP), a device thaty connects users to a wireless network, or a router.
